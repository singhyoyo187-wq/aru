Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions

For Fans

How do I book a session? +
Sign up or login → Browse creators → Select a creator → Choose a session type → Pick an available date and time slot → Pay securely via Razorpay. You will receive a confirmation email with your Zoom link immediately after payment.
What payment methods are accepted? +
We accept all major payment methods via Razorpay — UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), Credit/Debit cards, Net banking, and all major wallets. Payments are 100% secure and encrypted.
What is the 5% service charge? +
A small 5% service charge is added to every booking. This covers platform costs, payment processing, and customer support. It is shown clearly before you pay — no hidden charges.
Can I cancel my booking and get a refund? +
Yes — if you cancel at least 24 hours before your session, you get an 80% refund within 72 hours. If you cancel within 24 hours of the session, no refund is applicable. Go to Dashboard → My Bookings → Cancel booking.
What if the creator does not show up? +
If the creator is a no-show, you will receive a 100% full refund within 72 hours. No action needed from your side — just report it to us at sessionwala.bookings@gmail.com and we will process it immediately.
How do I join the video call? +
After booking, you will receive an email with your Zoom link. You can also find it in Dashboard → My Bookings. You can also chat with the creator directly from your booking card to get session details.
How do I chat with the creator? +
After your booking is confirmed, a Chat button appears on your booking card in the Dashboard. Tap it to open a private chat with your creator. You can share questions, get session prep tips, and receive the Zoom link directly.
I forgot my password. What do I do? +
Click Login → then click Forgot password? → enter your registered email address → you will receive a password reset link in your inbox. You can also change your password from Dashboard → Account → Change Password.
How do I leave a review? +
You can leave a review only after completing a paid session with a creator. Go to the creator's profile page and scroll to the Reviews section. Your review will be published after admin approval.

For Creators

How do I apply to join as a creator? +
Click Join as Creator on the homepage → fill in your details (name, email, Instagram handle, niche) → submit your application. Our team will review it within 2-5 business days and email you when approved.
Is there a fee to join SessionWala? +
No — joining SessionWala is completely free. For your first month, there is zero platform fee and you keep 100% of your session earnings (minus applicable GST). After the first month, a small platform fee applies.
How do I set my availability? +
After your profile is approved, go to Dashboard → My Slots. Use the Quick Add feature to select a day and pick multiple time slots at once. Fans can only book the slots you have added. You can add or remove slots anytime.
How do I communicate with fans before a session? +
Go to Dashboard → Messages to see all your confirmed bookings. Click on any booking to open a private chat with that fan. You can share the Zoom link, session preparation tips, and answer any questions directly in the chat.
How and when do I get paid? +
Payouts are processed every Monday via Razorpay to your registered bank account or UPI. Your earnings are shown in Dashboard → Earnings with a full breakdown including platform fee and GST deductions.
What happens if I cancel a session? +
If you cancel a confirmed booking, the fan receives a 100% refund and your next session earnings will be forfeited to SessionWala as a penalty. Please only accept bookings you can commit to.

General

Is SessionWala available across India? +
Yes — SessionWala is available across all of India. Sessions are conducted online via Zoom so location does not matter. Both fans and creators can be from anywhere in India.
Is my payment information safe? +
Yes — all payments are processed by Razorpay, which is PCI-DSS compliant and RBI regulated. SessionWala never stores your card or UPI details. Your data is secured on Google Firebase infrastructure.
