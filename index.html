Terms and Conditions

Last updated: January 2026

Welcome to LiveWith. By using our platform you agree to these terms.

1. Platform Overview

LiveWith is a marketplace connecting fans with creators for 1-on-1 live video sessions. We facilitate bookings and payments but are not responsible for session content.

2. Creator Terms

Application and Approval

All applications are reviewed manually. We reserve the right to approve or reject any application. Approved creators receive an email notification.

Pricing

Creators must list session prices inclusive of all applicable taxes including GST. LiveWith is not responsible for creator tax compliance.

Platform Fee and Payouts

First month: No platform fee. You keep 100% of your session price minus applicable GST deduction.

After first month: Platform fee applies. You will be notified before any fee is charged.

GST deduction: All payouts are processed after deduction of applicable GST as per Indian tax law. This applies even during your free first month.

Payout schedule: Every Monday via Razorpay to your registered bank account.

Cancellation by Creator

If a creator cancels a confirmed booking, the fan receives a 100% refund. The creator's next session earnings will be forfeited to LiveWith as penalty.

Creator No-Show

If a creator does not attend a confirmed session, the fan receives a full refund and the creator's next session earnings go entirely to LiveWith as penalty.

3. Fan Terms

Booking and Payment

A 5% service charge is added to all bookings at checkout. Payment is processed securely via Razorpay.

Cancellation and Refund Policy

Cancel 24+ hours before session: 80% refund within 72 hours to original payment method.

Cancel within 24 hours: No refund. Non-refundable for late cancellations.

Fan no-show: No refund. Profile will be flagged on the platform.

Creator no-show: 100% refund to you within 72 hours automatically.

Creator cancellation: 100% refund to you within 72 hours.

4. Reviews and Comments

Only fans who have completed a paid booking may leave a review. Reviews are subject to admin approval before being published. LiveWith reserves the right to remove any review that violates community guidelines.

5. Referral Programme

Creators earn LiveWith Credits by referring other creators or fans. Credits can be redeemed against platform fees. Credits have no cash value and cannot be transferred.

6. Prohibited Conduct

  • Providing false information in your profile or application
  • Attempting to conduct transactions outside the platform
  • Harassment or abuse of any user
  • Posting false or misleading reviews
  • Any illegal activity

7. Governing Law

These terms are governed by Indian law. Disputes are subject to courts in Delhi, India.
